About food

Chicken

Chicken is the culinary and industrial name for chicken meat. This type of poultry meat is the most common in the world. Due to the relative ease and low cost of raising chickens compared to animals such as cattle or pigs, chicken dominates many of the world's cuisines.

Beef

Beef, beef meat - the meat of domestic cows and bulls. The meat of young animals is called veal. In Western European cuisine, the meat of cows, bulls and oxen is strictly distinguished. In Russia, this is a general concept. People have been eating beef since prehistoric times. Beef is a source of high quality protein and nutrients

Sushi

Sushi, or sushi, is a traditional Japanese dish made from rice with vinegar seasoning and various seafood, as well as other ingredients. Since the early 1980s, sushi has gained widespread popularity in the West and around the world.
